Who We Are

We are Computer Engineering Undergraduates from ZHCET, Aligarh Muslim University. We've made this Application as a Minor Project during our V Semester. Experiencing the pandemic situation made us opt for this topic. These past few months made us realize how segregated the various classes of society are and how much more difficult this time has been for the lower classes as compared to us. Thus, our aim for this project was to use the technology at our disposal to bridge this gap between different to our best extent. Its purpose is to minimize the effort from an individual and to ease the act of charity, just as all technology aims at easing human life, we hope it fulfills its purpose. The app is open to any kind of improvement and changes (if any required at all) in the future. It is a simple solution to a problem that is frequently ignored and we hope that this project helps in bringing this issue to light.
